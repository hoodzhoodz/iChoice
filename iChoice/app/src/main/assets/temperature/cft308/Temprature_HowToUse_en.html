Quick Operation Guide

PLEASE KEEP FOR YOUR REFERENCE

Step 1 Power On:

Short press on "power" button to power on.

Step 2 Age Selection:

Set age range, the default age range is "36+"Months, one short press the temperature button, you could change to age range "3-36"Months, then "0-3"Months.

Step 3 Temperature Measurement:

Position between eyebrows up to 0.4in (≤1cm) away from your forehead, then press the temperature button, you could get your temperature after one beep sound in 1 second around.
